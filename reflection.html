Reflection

Our InterConnect project journey has been an exploration of innovation, empathy, and collaboration. In this reflection, we journey back through the process, drawing from our experiences, trials, setbacks, successes, and insights we have gathered.


Iteration Process:

The core of our InterConnect project was the continuous cycle of ideation, design, prototyping, demo, and feedback. Through a dynamic and iterative process, we gradually honed our ideas, shaping and reshaping them based on professor, client, and user feedback. Each iteration brought us one step closer to our ultimate goal: creating a tool that seamlessly blends the physical and virtual worlds of hybrid meetings.

Empathy-driven design:

The journey was an unwavering commitment to understanding the needs, desires, and challenges of our end-users, the 'Roomies' and 'Zoomies'. This deep-seated empathy formed the core of our design process, driving us to create a solution that ensured everyone, regardless of their location, felt connected, engaged, and valued.

As we conclude the project, the major takeaways are the iterative design process, dealing with miscommunications and understandings between the client and us, and being prepared for any future hurdles. InterConnect stays within our hearts, fuelling our anticipation for the promising future that awaits us.
